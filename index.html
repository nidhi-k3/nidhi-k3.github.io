About Me

I'm pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with an ECE focus and Theater Studies at Duke University (Class of 2026). I'm actively seeking hardware engineering opportunities where I can design, test, and optimize real systems.

I began my studies in Computer Science and quickly discovered I wanted to physically build as well as code. That led me to focus on embedded systems, sensor integration, and hardware design.

I've executed test plans, validated hardware performance under real-world conditions, and troubleshot complex integration challenges.

When I'm not building circuits, you'll find me on stage—I'm also studying Theater and have worked on technical production for multiple Duke Players shows.
